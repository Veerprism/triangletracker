Triangles Definitions

This is an equilateral triangle. An equilateral triangle is a triangle in which all three sides are equal.

An isosceles triangle is a triangle that has two sides of equal length. Sometimes it is specified as having exactly two sides of equal length, and sometimes as having at least two sides of equal length.

A scalene triangle is a triangle that has three unequal sides.

If the sum of any two sides of the triangle is equal to or less than the third remaining side, then a triangle CANNOT be formed using those values.
